What is Burst?

At Burst, we’re all about making social media fun again there is no annoying ads, and no weird algorithms controlling what you see. We want a place where you can post whatever you want, connect with real friends, and actually enjoy scrolling instead of feeling stressed.

Whether it’s memes, videos, or just random thoughts, Burst is your space to be yourself without all the drama. Let’s keep it real and make social media actually social.

Key Features

  • No ads
  • No algorithm feed control
  • Post anything: memes, videos, thoughts
  • Connect with real friends
